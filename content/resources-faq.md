# FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is ROKO?**  
A: ROKO is the world's first temporal blockchain with nanosecond precision, preventing MEV and ensuring fair transaction ordering.

**Q: How does temporal consensus work?**  
A: Validators synchronize atomic clocks to achieve nanosecond precision, creating deterministic transaction ordering based on true time.

**Q: What is a NanoMoment?**  
A: A NanoMoment is our unit of temporal measurement, representing one nanosecond on the blockchain.
